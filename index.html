Getting Advice from Appropriately Qualified Migration Agents

We are highly qualified and registered Australian Migration Agents. The Principal is Peter Rozsy, who together with a team of experienced registered Migration Agents advise and submit visa applications on your behalf. You can be assured of an exceptional level of service.

It is an Australian Government legal requirement that only registered Migration Agents give migration advice. A Migration Agent is a person who is registered with the Australian Government’s Migration Agents Registration Authority (MARA). Peter Rozsy’s MARA registration number is 9901480.

MARA provide two documents that you should look at before appointing us to assist you. You can read the Code of Conduct for registered migration agents here. You can read the Consumer Guide here.
